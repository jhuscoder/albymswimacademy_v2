ABOUT US

Founded in 2018, Albyn Swim Academy has been saving lives by providing swimming lessons for learners of all ages throughout Abuja and Nigeria. Offering year-round lessons, our family-run company operates the only indoor heated swim academy in the country. Backed by many years of experience and professionally accredited instructors, our mission is to raise awareness of water safety, save lives, and elevate every swimmer with precision coaching, data‑driven progress, and a supportive team culture

We are a private Swim academy with an indoor heated pool that teaches water safety and survival skills and learning to swim from babies 6 months old to adults of all ages.

Our mission is to elevate every swimmer with precision coaching, data‑driven progress, and a supportive team culture.

We are Certified

We’re proud to be connected with trusted organizations that share our passion for water safety, professional training, and global swimming standards.

World Aquatics

Recognized by World Aquatics formally known as FINA, the sole and exclusive international governing body for aquatic sports.

Aquatic Federation of Nigeria

Accredited federation membership promoting swimming excellence, water safety awareness, and aquatic sports development nationwide.

Swimkidz SA

International learn-to-swim partner specialising in early aquatic development programs for babies, children, and adults across South Africa.

Starfish Aquatics Institute (SAI)

Independent Authorized Provider of Starfish Swimming®, a nationally recognized swim instruction curriculum developed by the Starfish Aquatics Institute (SAI).
