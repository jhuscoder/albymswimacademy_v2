Swimming Programs

Give your baby, child, or yourself the lifelong gift of confident swimming in our heated indoor pools right here in Abuja. Our state-of-the-art facility offers personalized lessons for all ages, running Monday to Sunday, all year round, so progress never pauses.

Swimming Programs

Structured pathways for all ages and skill levels, designed to build confidence, technique, and endurance.

Babies & Toddlers

Gentle, safety-first classes to help infants and toddlers build confidence, coordination, and a love for the water.

Children & Teenagers

Technique, endurance, and mobility-focused lessons for all skill levels, helping young swimmers progress confidently.

Adult Lessons

Structured adult programs with drills, technique work, and fitness training to enhance performance and confidence in the water.

Private Sessions

One-on-one coaching tailored to your goals, perfect for focused skill improvement, swim technique, or competitive training.
