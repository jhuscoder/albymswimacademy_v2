About Us

About Albyn Swim Academy Ltd

Albyn Swim Academy Ltd is a swimming school that serves babies, children, and adults throughout Nigeria. Based in Abuja, we offer swimming and water safety lessons all year round at our heated indoor swimming pools. When you choose our lessons, instructors pay close attention to your progress, working at your pace and ability and ensuring you are comfortable while in the water. Get in touch today for more details.

Our Tutors

We employ professional swimming instructors to help learners with all aspects of swimming and water safety. All our tutors are professionally trained and accredited by Starfish Aquatics Institute (SAI), and we work closely with learners to understand their needs and help them make progress.

Our Purpose

The main purpose of starting Albyn Swim Academy Ltd is to cut back the death rate from people drowning. Nigeria is one of the top 50 countries on the planet for deaths caused by drowning. At our school, we teach babies, children, and adults to swim with an emphasis on water safety, thereby reducing the number of inexperienced swimmers and, in turn, lowering the death rate in the country.

Our Affiliations

Swimkidz SA

Established in 2006, SwimkidzSA trains babies, kids, and adult learners with swimming lessons throughout South Africa. Specialising in early learning, they offer a variety of lessons, from the 'Learn to Swim' program to advanced swimming lessons. With more than 10 years of experience, our sister company caters to all ages.

Aquatic Federation of Nigeria

As members of the Aquatic Federation of Nigeria, we work to enhance water safety and improve the image of swimming in the country.
