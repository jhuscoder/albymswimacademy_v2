About Albyn Swim Academy

Albyn Swim Academy Ltd is a leading swimming school serving babies, children, and adults across Nigeria. We combine expert coaching, water-safety education, and a supportive learning environment to help every swimmer build confidence and life-saving skills.

Enroll Today

Our Story

Albyn Swim Academy Ltd was founded with a clear mission — to equip individuals of all ages with essential swimming skills while promoting water safety across Nigeria. Recognizing the growing need for structured swimming education, we created a supportive environment where babies, children, and adults can learn confidently at their own pace.

Based in Abuja, our academy offers year-round lessons in heated indoor pools, guided by professionally trained instructors. We focus not only on technique but also on building confidence, discipline, and lifelong fitness habits.

Today, Albyn Swim Academy continues to impact lives by helping learners overcome fear, develop strength, and gain life-saving skills that stay with them forever.

Our Purpose

Our mission is to reduce drowning incidents and build confident swimmers by providing high-quality instruction, water safety awareness, and a supportive learning environment for people of all ages.

Water Safety First

We teach essential safety skills that help prevent drowning and prepare swimmers to respond confidently in water environments.

Confidence Through Learning

Our structured programs help swimmers overcome fear, improve ability, and build confidence at every stage of their journey.

Saving Lives Through Education

By teaching swimming and awareness, we aim to reduce drowning rates and create safer communities across Nigeria.

Meet Our Team

We employ professional swimming instructors to help learners with all aspects of swimming and water safety. All our tutors are professionally trained and accredited by Starfish Aquatics Institute (SAI), and we work closely with learners to understand their needs and help them make progress.

Our Affiliations

We’re proud to be connected with trusted organizations that share our passion for water safety, professional training, and global swimming standards.

Swimkidz SA

Established in 2006, SwimkidzSA trains babies, kids, and adult learners with swimming lessons throughout South Africa. Specialising in early learning, they offer a variety of lessons, from the 'Learn to Swim' program to advanced swimming lessons. With more than 10 years of experience, our sister company caters to all ages.

Aquatic Federation of Nigeria

As members of the Aquatic Federation of Nigeria, we work to enhance water safety and improve the image of swimming in the country.
