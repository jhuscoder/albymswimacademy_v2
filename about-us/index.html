About Albyn Swim Academy

Albyn Swim Academy Ltd is a swimming school that serves babies, children, and adults throughout Nigeria. Based in Abuja, we offer swimming and water safety lessons all year round at our heated indoor swimming pools. When you choose our lessons, instructors pay close attention to your progress, working at your pace and ability and ensuring you are comfortable while in the water. Our Tutors

We employ professional swimming instructors to help learners with all aspects of swimming and water safety. All our tutors are professionally trained and accredited by Starfish Aquatics Institute (SAI), and we work closely with learners to understand their needs and help them make progress.

Our Purpose

Our mission is to raise awareness of water safety, save lives, and elevate every swimmer with precision coaching, data‑driven progress, and a supportive team culture

Water Safety First

We teach essential safety skills that help prevent drowning and prepare swimmers to respond confidently in real water environments.

Confidence Through Learning

Structured programs help swimmers overcome fear, improve ability, and build lasting confidence at every stage of development.

Saving Lives Through Education

By teaching swimming awareness and safety education, we aim to reduce drowning rates and create safer communities across Nigeria.
